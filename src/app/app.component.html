About Us

Eyelids,was born to give kids a attitude of togetherness and companionship with soceity.Started in 2000 in Basaveshwarnagar in the name called kids cottage which later shifted to Vidyaranyapura in 2005 in the name of 'eyelids'.

In Eyelids every child is taken care with atmost love and affection . We believe that a child will know and understand things when they are allowed to explore freely.

MISSION

Eyelids aims at education through freedom of exploration of things.It is a kids zone where they have given complete freedom to play around and get the feel of everything around. here kids grow with more confidence and compassion towards the fellow being .Every aspect of communication on moral values is being taught.
